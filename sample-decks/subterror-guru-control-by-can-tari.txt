# Subterror Guru Control #
##########################


# An easy to play going-first deck that focuses on Subterror Guru to slowly generate advantage.
# The small size of the engine allows for many hand traps, floodgates and other traps.

# Piloted by Can Tari at YCS Düsseldorf 1 (2019-02-23 and 24), reached top 8.
# Source: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SNiZYcQ0JkI

# Watch the coverage of YCS Düsseldorf 1 (2019) to see this deck in action:
# https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Xr-Au-f09rQ
